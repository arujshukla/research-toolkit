\section{Empirical Strategy}

This section can house identification arguments, estimating equations, assumptions, and specification details.

A standard baseline specification might be:
\begin{align}
  y_{it} = \alpha + \beta x_{it} + \gamma_t + \delta_i + \varepsilon_{it}.
\end{align}

Here, $\gamma_t$ denotes time fixed effects and $\delta_i$ denotes unit fixed effects. The estimating equation is intentionally simple in the template so it can be adapted quickly.
